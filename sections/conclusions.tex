\section{Conclusions and Future Work}
The experimental results showed that developing the SudoSLVRR solver helps speed up the search in very large solution space. The proposed solver achieved near-perfect solution success rate and showed a huge reduction in execution time. Placing the integration of constraint propagation and dynamic collaborative mechanism of multi-colony ant optimization inside each thread that communicates periodically creates a robust framework that reduces exponential time increase when faced with complex constraint satisfaction problems (CSP). 

Future work may explore adapting the multithreaded multi-colony framework to highly constrained problems like university timetabling and job-shop scheduling.
